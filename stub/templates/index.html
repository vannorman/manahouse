FAQ

Included in retreat:

  • Accommodations for 2 nights
  • Prepared dinners, ingredients for breakfast and lunch
  • Yoga classes 1-2 times a day
  • Guided meditation
  • Wi-fi in all rooms and common areas
  • Access to self-guided hiking trail within tropical rainforest
  • Use of surfboards and snorkeling gear
  • Retreat coordination

Not included in retreat:

  • Airfare to/from Puerto Rico
  • Travel insurance

All levels of yoga experience are welcome, from beginners to more seasoned practitioners. Come as you are—your practice is your own!

This region has a tropical climate. Daily high temperatures are around 83°F (rarely falling below 80°F or exceeding 86°F). Daily low temperatures are around 70°F (rarely falling below 67°F or exceeding 72°F). Though February is the month with the least rainfall, you may get some precipitation during the retreat.

Dinners will be made right in the retreat kitchen and catered. There will be ingredients for breakfast and lunch to be prepared as a group. Special dietary needs such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten free, and allergies can be accommodated. Please let us know when you book your retreat.

We have enough beds to sleep 12, and with cots we can sleep an additional 10. There is a minimum of six persons required to run a retreat. Many guests are traveling solo. Please feel very welcome to come on your own. It will be an incredible experience!
